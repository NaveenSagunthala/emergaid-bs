About this EmergAid:

LIFE IS PRECIOUS
Your Pocket First-Aid Companion
EmergAid is your trusted mobile application designed to equip you with the knowledge and confidence to act effectively in medical emergencies. We understand that critical moments reqire clear guidance, and that's why EmergAid prioritizes offline accessibility and rich media content, empowering you to take action.

Our comprehensive app features:

step-by-step instruction:
EmergAid guides you through first-aid procedures with clear, concies instructions that are easy to follow even in stressful situations.
Visual identification aids:
Don't be confused! EmergAid incorporates image to help you identify injuries and conditions quickly.
Detailed procedures:
Gain a deeper understanding of first-aid techniques with comprehensive explanations for each step.
Graphical references:
Enhance your learning with informative charts and diagrams for better visual understanding.
Emergency helplines:
Get immediate access to crucial phone numbers for emergency services like ambulances and poison control centers.
Nearest hospital locator:
Find the closest medical facility with our integrated hospital locator, ensuring timely care for the injured.
WHO first-aid video demonstrations:
Learn lifesaving techniques through clear and informative video demonstrations provided by the World Health Organization WHO.
